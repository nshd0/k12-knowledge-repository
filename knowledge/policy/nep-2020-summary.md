# NEP 2020 — Key Principles and Structure

## Vision

NEP 2020 envisions an education system rooted in Indian ethos that contributes directly
to transforming India into an equitable and vibrant knowledge society.

## Curricular Structure — 5+3+3+4

| Stage | Grades | Ages | Focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| Foundational | Pre-school + G1–2 | 3–8 | Play-based, activity-based learning |
| Preparatory | G3–5 | 8–11 | Gradual shift to formal learning |
| Middle | G6–8 | 11–14 | Subject teachers, critical thinking |
| Secondary | G9–12 | 14–18 | Multidisciplinary, flexibility |

## Key Reforms

- No hard separation of arts, sciences, and commerce streams at secondary stage
- Mother tongue / home language as medium of instruction up to Grade 5 where possible
- Competency-based assessment replacing rote-recall testing
- 50% of time in vocational education for all students by 2025
- Foundational Literacy and Numeracy (FLN) as national mission (NIPUN Bharat)
