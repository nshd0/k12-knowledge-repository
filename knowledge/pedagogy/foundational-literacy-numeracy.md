# NIPUN Bharat — Foundational Literacy and Numeracy Mission

## Mission Goal

Every child in India achieves basic reading, writing, and arithmetic competencies
by end of Grade 3 by the year 2026–27.

## Key Milestones (per MoE guidelines)

**By end of Grade 1:**  
- Reads simple words and sentences  
- Counts and writes numbers up to 99  
- Performs basic addition and subtraction

**By end of Grade 2:**  
- Reads short paragraphs with comprehension  
- Understands place value up to 999  
- Performs 2-digit addition/subtraction with carrying

**By end of Grade 3:**  
- Reads grade-level text with fluency and comprehension  
- Solves simple multiplication and division problems  
- Understands fractions conceptually

## Teaching Approaches

- Structured pedagogy with decodable texts
- Play-based and activity-based learning
- Mother-tongue instruction in early grades
- Regular assessment using FLN checklists
