# NCF-SE 2023 — School Education Framework Summary

## Overview

The National Curriculum Framework for School Education 2023 (NCF-SE 2023) replaces
NCF 2005. It provides the curricular and pedagogical framework for all four stages
defined in NEP 2020.

## Key Principles

- Learning should be meaningful, joyful, and connected to real life
- Assessment should be holistic and competency-based, not high-stakes only
- Teachers are central — professional development and autonomy are emphasised
- Multidisciplinarity across subjects from the Preparatory stage onwards

## Stage-wise Learning Areas

**Foundational:** Language, Mathematics, Art, Movement, World around us  
**Preparatory:** Language, Mathematics, Art, EVS, Social Studies  
**Middle:** Languages, Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Arts, PE, Vocational  
**Secondary:** Core + elective subjects with flexibility across domains

## Holistic Progress Card (HPC)

NCF-SE 2023 recommends replacing traditional mark sheets with HPCs that capture
cognitive, social, physical, and creative development indicators.
