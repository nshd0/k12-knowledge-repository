# AI Layer Personas

## Principal
Needs compliance alerts, circular summaries, deadline tracking, and policy change briefings.

## Teacher
Needs practical pedagogy strategies, lesson planning resources, assessment tools, and training recommendations.

## Student
Needs subject-wise notes, revision material, sample papers, and exam prep resources.

## Parent
Needs clear and simple summaries of circulars, academic calendar reminders, and school updates.

## Coordinator
Needs dashboard views, source status tracking, update logs, and weekly digests.
