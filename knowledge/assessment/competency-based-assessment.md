# Competency-Based Assessment — PARAKH Framework

## What is PARAKH?

PARAKH (Performance Assessment, Review and Analysis of Knowledge for Holistic
Development) is MoE's national body for setting norms for student assessment,
replacing the earlier marks-only model.

## Core Principles

- Assess what students can **do** with knowledge, not just what they recall
- Use a mix of formative (ongoing) and summative (periodic) assessments
- Align with learning outcomes defined in NCF-SE 2023
- Reduce high-stakes exam anxiety through continuous assessment

## Assessment Types

| Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Formative | Ongoing (daily/weekly) | Guide teaching, not for grading |
| Periodic | Monthly/quarterly | Track progress, inform planning |
| Summative | End of term/year | Certify achievement levels |
