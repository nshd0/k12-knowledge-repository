## What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence, or AI, is the ability of a computer or machine to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence.

Think about what you do every day:
- You recognize your friends' faces.
- You understand when your teacher speaks in Hindi or English.
- You make decisions like "Should I carry an umbrella today?"

AI teaches computers to do similar things.

> **Simple Definition:** AI is when a machine learns from data and uses that learning to make decisions or complete tasks — just like how you learn from experience.

## How Does AI Learn?

Imagine you want to teach a small child to identify a cat.
- You show them 100 pictures of cats and say “This is a cat.”
- You show them 100 pictures of dogs and say “This is NOT a cat.”
- After seeing many examples, the child starts recognizing cats on their own.

AI learns the same way — by looking at thousands of examples (called **data**) and finding patterns.

This process is called **Machine Learning** — it is one of the most important branches of AI.

---

## Types of AI Tasks

AI can be trained to do many different types of tasks:

1. **Seeing** — recognizing objects, faces, or text in images (Image Recognition)
2. **Listening** — understanding spoken words (Speech Recognition)
3. **Reading** — understanding written language (Natural Language Processing)
4. **Deciding** — making choices based on patterns (Decision Making)
5. **Creating** — writing text, generating images, composing music (Generative AI)

## Human Intelligence vs Artificial Intelligence

| Ability | Human | AI |
|------------------------------|-------|----|
| Learns from experience | Yes | Yes (with data) |
| Feels emotions | Yes | No |
| Gets tired | Yes | No |
| Processes millions of items | No | Yes |
| Common sense reasoning | Yes | Improving |
| Creativity | Yes | Improving |

> **Key Insight:** AI is very powerful for specific tasks, but humans still excel at creativity, empathy, and common sense.

## Key Vocabulary

| Term | Meaning |
|----------------------|---------------------------------------------------------------|
| Artificial Intelligence | The ability of machines to perform tasks requiring human intelligence |
| Data | Information used to train AI systems |
| Machine Learning | A way for computers to learn from data without being explicitly programmed |
| Algorithm | A set of step-by-step instructions a computer follows |
| Pattern Recognition | AI’s ability to identify patterns in data |

## Summary

- AI stands for Artificial Intelligence — machines that can think and learn.
- AI is all around us: in phones, apps, search engines, and maps.
- AI learns from large amounts of data called training data.
- Machine Learning is when computers learn from examples.
- AI is great at specific tasks; humans still lead in creativity and emotions.
