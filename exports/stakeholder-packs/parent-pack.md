## Understanding the School System

### National Education Policy 2020
**ID:** FW-001  
The NEP 2020 transforms the school system. Key change for parents: schools now follow a
5+3+3+4 structure (Foundational → Preparatory → Middle → Secondary) instead of the old
10+2 structure. Emphasis shifts from rote learning to understanding and skills.  
**Link:** https://www.education.gov.in/sites/upload_files/mhrd/files/NEP_2020.pdf

### NCF School Education 2023
**ID:** FW-002  
The NCF-SE 2023 is the curriculum framework your child's school follows. It defines
what is taught at each stage and how children should be assessed.  
**Link:** https://ncert.nic.in/focus-group.php?ln=hi

### NCF Foundational Stage 2022
**ID:** FW-003  
Covers Pre-school through Grade 2 (ages 3–8). Play-based learning is the primary method.  
**Link:** https://ncert.nic.in/focus-group.php?ln=hi

## Assessment and Progress

### PARAKH Assessment Framework
**ID:** AS-002  
Explains how competency-based assessment works — your child is assessed on what they
can do, not just what they recall. Reports now include multiple dimensions of growth.  
**Link:** https://parakh.gov.in

### Learning Outcomes NCF 2023
**ID:** AS-005  
Grade-wise list of what your child is expected to know and be able to do by the end
of each grade.  
**Link:** https://ncert.nic.in

### NIPUN Bharat
**ID:** AS-001 | Grades 1–3  
If your child is in Grades 1–3, their school tracks Foundational Literacy and Numeracy
milestones under this national mission.  
**Link:** https://nipunbharat.education.gov.in

### CBSE Exam Date Sheet and Schedule
**ID:** CR-005 | Grades 10 and 12  
Official board exam schedule and dates from CBSE.  
**Link:** https://www.cbse.gov.in
